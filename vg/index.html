About Me

VICKY

Victoria Georgieva, known professionally as VICTORIA. Is a Bulgarian singer and songwriter. By this moment she has 7 official songs. Victoria begun singing at the age of 11 when she enrolled in the vocal studio "Angel Voices" with music teacher Atanaska Lipcheva. “ I remember that it was a very popular series "Hannah Montana" and I watch it all the time. One day I decided I wanted to be like her and she I enrolled in singing lessons. That's how it all starts.“
