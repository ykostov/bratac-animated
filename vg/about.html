A short story of my life

Victoria Georgieva, known professionally as VICTORIA. Is a Bulgarian singer and songwriter. By this moment she has 7 official songs. Victoria begun singing at the age of 11 when she enrolled in the vocal studio "Angel Voices" with music teacher Atanaska Lipcheva. “ I remember that it was a very popular series "Hannah Montana" and I watch it all the time. One day I decided I wanted to be like her and she I enrolled in singing lessons. That's how it all starts.“ In 2012, when she was 14 years old, she auditioned for the first time in the bulgarian music competition X Factor, but she was declined mostly for her age. However she didn’t give up, and after 3 unsuccessful attempts she finally got approved in the X Factor season 4 in the year 2015 (at the age of 17). After all she ended the competition as number 6 at week 6 (from 12 weeks), but that was just the beginning. Soon after she got an offer to join Virginia Records, but she declined it. Then she accept an offer from MonteMusic. With them she made her first songs - “Nisto Slucaino” ft Niki Bakalov and VenZy in 2016, “Nezavurshen Roman” in 2016 and “Chast ot Men” in 2017. But during her stay in the label Victoria couldn’t reveal and find herself at 100%, so she left them. The year after, as independent artist, she made the song “Stranni Vremena”. In 2018 Vicky spent 10 days in LA, where she made and filmed her first English song “I wanna know”. For a first time she actually participated in the making of the lyrics. Director was Dimitry Stefanov, and the executive producer was Velina Ilieva. That was her breakthrough into the international music scene and the video already has more than 1M views! Soon after it was revealed that Victoria will represent her country Bulgaria in the Eurovision 2020! She and her sponsor LigmaGroup made the song “Tears Getting Sober” – which tells about the inner difficulties in people – a topic which is very close to Victoria. Unfortunately, because of the Covid-19, the Eurovision 2020 was canceled, but Victoria still will represent Bulgaria in Eurovision 2021 with a new song. In the summer of 2020, Vicky announce her song “Alright” – created in a Eurovision camps. The song carries a message of hope and a new beginning and it was filmed in the seasides of the town Burgas again by LigmaGroup. "Alright is dedicated to people who face daily difficulties and are constantly looking for the good in themselves," said Victoria, who is also co-director in the video.
